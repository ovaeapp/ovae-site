A daily companion

A short message each morning that knows where you are in your cycle or stage — plus a 15-second log and predictions that are honest about uncertainty.

Partner mode

Your person sees only what you choose to share — your phase, your day, and one way to show up — never your symptoms or history. You approve the connection; you can end it any time.

Partners can send comfort to your door in a few taps — and requests you make land on their side as one-tap jobs.

No ads, no trackers, no data selling. Encrypted on your phone, identity stored separately from health data, and one tap deletes everything — provably. The promise →
