Privacy is the product

Built like it matters. Because it does.

No ads. No trackers. No analytics SDKs. No data selling. Ever. Your health data is encrypted on your phone, your identity is stored separately from your health history, and “delete everything” means everything — one tap erases your account and every trace of it, provably.

Predictions are honest, too: when your cycle runs late, Ovae says “you're running late — that's within normal” instead of inventing a phantom day 2. An app you trust with this data shouldn't guess out loud.
